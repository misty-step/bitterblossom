## Research Findings
- ADR-003 is the governing design: GitHub is the system of record, the conductor runs remotely, and governance is separate from building.
- The highest-value backlog is not more CLI surface; it is durable orchestration state, routing quality, isolation, and observability.
- Every surviving issue now fits one of two buckets: control-plane hardening or thin transport hardening in service of the control plane.
- The repo is missing tune-repo context artifacts entirely, so durable context regeneration is now an explicit backlog item (#475).
